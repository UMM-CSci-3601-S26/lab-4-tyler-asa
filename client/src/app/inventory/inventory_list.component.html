INVENTORY

This page lists the organization's current inventory, including both stocked and desired items.
Click on any item to modify its properties or remove it from inventory. Click the 'Add Item' button to add new items to the inventory.
The inventory can be sorted by various properties. Use the 'Sort By' section to change the order of items. When by type, items will be subdivided by category.
When restocking, use the 'Reset Locations' button to remove locations from all items. (This CANNOT be undone!)
