Supply List

Welcome to the Supply List page! Here, you can view the items a student in each grade at each school needs, as well as filter them by various attributes. In the future, functionality for generating supply sheets and other similar features may be added!

Use the fields below to filter the inventory by school, grade, item, brand, color, size, type, and material. The filters will be applied as you type, and you can clear them all at once using the "Clear" button on the bottom.
